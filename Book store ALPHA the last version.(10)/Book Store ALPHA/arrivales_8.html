Diary of a Wimpy Kid: Squid Game

About This Book

Author: Jeff Kinney (fan parody)  |  Genre: Comedy, Parody, Children's Fiction

This fan-made parody mashup combines the beloved Diary of a Wimpy Kid series with the cultural phenomenon of Squid Game. Greg Heffley finds himself in the most ridiculous and dangerously competitive situation of his middle-school life, all while trying to maintain his reputation and survive the games.

Filled with the same humor, relatable awkwardness, and diary-style illustrations that made Wimpy Kid a global hit, this parody imagines what would happen if Greg had to compete in a series of outrageous childhood games with very serious consequences.

Note: This is a creative fan parody cover. The official Diary of a Wimpy Kid series is published by Jeff Kinney and Amulet Books. Always support official authors and editions.
