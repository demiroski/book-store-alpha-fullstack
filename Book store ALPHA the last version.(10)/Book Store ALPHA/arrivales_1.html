The Giver

About This Book

Author: Lois Lowry  |  Genre: Dystopian Fiction, Young Adult, Science Fiction

The Giver is a Newbery Medal-winning novel set in a seemingly perfect society where pain, war, and conflict have been eliminated. The story follows twelve-year-old Jonas, who is selected to receive memories of the true past from an elderly man known only as The Giver.

As Jonas discovers the dark truth behind his community's false sense of peace, he is forced to confront questions about freedom, individuality, and the cost of a controlled society. The novel explores what it means to truly feel and to truly live.

A timeless classic in young adult literature, The Giver challenges readers to think critically about conformity, choice, and the importance of human emotion and memory.
