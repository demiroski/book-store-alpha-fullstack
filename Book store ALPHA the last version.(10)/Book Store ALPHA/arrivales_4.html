Red Queen

About This Book

Author: Victoria Aveyard  |  Genre: Fantasy, Young Adult, Dystopian

Red Queen is set in a world divided by blood — Silver bloods rule with superhuman abilities, while Red bloods live as second-class citizens. Mare Barrow, a poor Red girl, discovers she possesses a power of her own, a power that shouldn't exist in someone like her.

Thrust into the Silver world as a disguised princess, Mare must navigate palace intrigue, dangerous alliances, and a brewing revolution. Power is a dangerous game, and in this world, anyone can betray anyone.

Filled with twists, romance, and political tension, Red Queen is a gripping debut that launches an epic series about inequality, identity, and the courage to fight for what is right.
