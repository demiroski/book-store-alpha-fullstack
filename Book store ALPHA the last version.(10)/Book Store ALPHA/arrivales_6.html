Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

About This Book

Author: J.K. Rowling  |  Genre: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone is the first book in the beloved Harry Potter series. It introduces us to Harry, an orphaned boy who discovers on his eleventh birthday that he is a wizard. He is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where an extraordinary world of magic, friendship, and mystery awaits him.

At Hogwarts, Harry makes loyal friends, faces dangerous challenges, and begins to uncover the truth about his mysterious past and the dark wizard who killed his parents. The story is full of wonder, humor, and heart.

One of the best-selling book series of all time, Harry Potter has captured the imaginations of readers of all ages around the world and continues to inspire generations of new fans.
