Ranger's Apprentice: The Ruins of Gorlan

About This Book

Author: John Flanagan  |  Genre: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

The Ruins of Gorlan is the first book in the internationally bestselling Ranger's Apprentice series. Will, a young orphan hoping to become a knight, is instead chosen as an apprentice to the mysterious and legendary Ranger Halt. Rangers are the silent protectors of the kingdom, skilled in archery, tracking, and stealth.

As Will trains and discovers his own hidden strengths, a great evil stirs in the ruins of Gorlan. Two of the most dangerous creatures in the land have been unleashed, and it falls to Will and Halt to protect the kingdom before it is too late.

A thrilling tale of adventure, friendship, and courage, The Ruins of Gorlan is the perfect start to one of the most beloved fantasy series for young readers.
