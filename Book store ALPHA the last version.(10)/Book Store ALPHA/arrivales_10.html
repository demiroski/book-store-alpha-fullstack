Percy Jackson and the Lightning Thief

About This Book

Author: Rick Riordan  |  Genre: Fantasy, Adventure, Greek Mythology, Young Adult

Percy Jackson and the Lightning Thief is the debut novel in the massively popular Percy Jackson & the Olympians series. Percy is a troubled twelve-year-old who has been expelled from multiple schools and can never seem to stay out of trouble. On a school trip, he is attacked by a mythological creature and discovers that he is the son of a Greek god.

Thrust into the world of Mount Olympus, Percy is accused of stealing Zeus's master lightning bolt. With the help of his friends Annabeth and Grover, he embarks on a cross-country quest to find the bolt and prevent a war among the gods.

Funny, fast-paced, and full of heart, Percy Jackson and the Lightning Thief is a modern mythology classic that has inspired a generation of readers to discover the magic of ancient Greek myths through an entirely new lens.
