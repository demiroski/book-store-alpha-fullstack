Heroes of Olympus: The Lost Hero

About This Book

Author: Rick Riordan  |  Genre: Fantasy, Adventure, Greek Mythology, Young Adult

The Lost Hero is the first book in the Heroes of Olympus series, a sequel series to the beloved Percy Jackson saga. The story follows Jason Grace, who wakes up on a school bus with no memory of who he is, alongside his friends Piper and Leo. Together, they are sent on a dangerous quest involving the gods of Olympus.

The book introduces new demigods and blends Greek and Roman mythology in exciting ways, expanding the Percy Jackson universe with fresh characters and even higher stakes. Old fans and new readers will be equally gripped by the action and humor.

A fast-paced, richly imagined adventure, The Lost Hero is a perfect entry point into Rick Riordan's expanded mythological world.
