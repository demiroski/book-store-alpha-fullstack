The Wright Brothers

About This Book

Author: David McCullough  |  Genre: Non-Fiction, Biography, History

From the Pulitzer Prize-winning author David McCullough comes the dramatic story of the Wright Brothers — Orville and Wilbur — two humble bicycle mechanics from Dayton, Ohio who changed the world forever. With no formal engineering education and almost no financial backing, they achieved what many thought impossible.

McCullough brings the brothers to life through meticulous research and compelling storytelling. The book captures not only their remarkable invention but also the character, dedication, and unwavering belief that drove them to success against enormous odds.

A tribute to American ingenuity and perseverance, The Wright Brothers is an inspiring account of two men who dared to dream of flight and made it a reality for all of humanity.
